FORGETFUL WIFE

After eating, when she got in the car and went quite far, the wife
suddenly remembered that she forgot her glasses on the dining table.

On the way back to the shop, the husband always grumbled about his wife's forgetfulness.

Arriving at the old place, when the wife rushed in to find glasses, the husband told him to follow:
- Don't forget to bring me both my hat and umbrella!
